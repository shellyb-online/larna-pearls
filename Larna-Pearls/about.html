At GreenHeart, we are Accelerating Regeneration and helping farmers to Thrive

At GreenHeart, we're revolutionizing sustainable agriculture with innovative biochar solutions. We are here to empower farmers with regenerative alternatives that enhance soil and animal health, increase crop yields, and mitigate climate change.

Our Approach - We Nurture Regeneration

We repurpose agricultural and forestry waste to generate high-quality carbon negative product to build a more regenerative future and help farmers to thrive. We do this by working closely with nature and clean technology to enable ecosystems function at optimum best and address food insecurity, planetary and human health, and climate change.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At GreenHeart, sustainability is at the core of everything we do. Our production processes are eco-conscious, utilizing renewable resources and minimizing environmental impact. By upcycling agricultural residues into high-quality biochar, we're closing the loop on waste and promoting a circular economy.

Our Innovative Products

Biochar Soil Conditioner

Biochar is a carbon-rich, porous material produced by heating organic waste in the absence of oxygen. It significantly improves soil fertility, moisture retention, and nutrient availability while supporting beneficial microbial life. By locking carbon in the soil for hundreds of years, biochar not only enhances agricultural productivity but also plays a vital role in climate change mitigation and environmental restoration.

Biochar-Based Animal Feed

AniCARE can be fed continuously as a natural detoxifier of the digestive system supporting the animal's metabolic function by helping maintain a natural acid-alkaline balance. It improves digestion and reduces methane emissions in livestock.Increase fertility, milk production (for ruminants), egg production (for birds), growth rates, feed conversion ratio and total productivity. Improve overall animal health and reduce illness and infection

BioCHARGE

GreenHeart's BioCharge is a natural liquid byproduct, collected during the biochar process to offer a chemical-free product. It contains a complex mixture of organic compounds, including acetic acid, phenols, and other volatile substances. Wood vinegar is utilized in agriculture as a fertilizer, pesticide, and plant growth enhancer.

ASASEHENE SOIL CONDITIONER (NEW)

ASASEhene Soil Conditioner is a nutrient-rich blend of compost and biochar created through thermophilic composting. It enhances soil health by improving nutrient availability, water retention, and biological activity. Ideal for all soil and plant types, ASASEhene boosts crop quality, strengthens soil structure, and helps prevent pests and soil-borne diseases—supporting healthier, more productive farming.

Mission
