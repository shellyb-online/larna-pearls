Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a global symbol of grace, strength, and purpose through high-quality pearl jewelry that transcends adornment offering meaning, ministry, and timeless beauty to every wearer.

Mission

At LARNA, our mission is to create handcrafted and curated pearl pieces that speak to the soul—celebrating identity, heritage, and faith. Through thoughtful design and spiritual depth, we aim to turn every piece into a personal prayer of Grit, Grace, and Greatness.

Our Leadership

Annie Joy - Founder & CEO

I started out as a writer. Then I became a journalist, practiced for years, transitioned into marketing, and eventually stepped into real estate. But in February 2024, while planning the wardrobe for a wedding I had helped organize, my sister-in-law and I couldn’t find jewelry that truly stood out. That search sparked a conversation—one that planted the first seed of what would later become LARNA.

Some months later, just before my birthday in July, a moment of reflection rekindled something I had forgotten: my childhood love for pearls. I remembered wearing my mother’s pearls with pride, not knowing where they came from but knowing how they made me feel—elegant, special, deeply seen.

With LARNA, I set out to create that same feeling—jewelry that holds meaning, evokes grace, and feels deeply personal. Every piece reflects not just craftsmanship, but a piece of my story, my heritage, and my hope to preserve the elegance of pearls for generations to come.

LARNA is also an expression of my ministry as an ambassador for Christ. So, when you wear LARNA, you’re not just wearing jewelry. You’re wearing grit, grace, and prayer.
That’s what LARNA is to me: a testimony of faith, a symbol of strength, and a quiet reminder that beauty can carry purpose.
