AniCARE 360: Keeping Livestock Healthy Naturally

AniCARE 360 is GreenHeart SE's innovative solution for natural animal care. This feed enhancer harnesses the power of biochar and natural minerals to detoxify livestock digestive systems, improve performance, and reduce environmental impact.

Healthier Animals, Healthier Planet

By adding AniCARE 360 to animal feed, farmers help their livestock absorb nutrients more efficiently and reduce harmful emissions. The biochar binds toxins and supports gut health, leading to stronger, healthier animals and cleaner farms.

Environmental Benefits

Livestock farming is a major source of greenhouse gases. AniCARE 360 helps lower methane and ammonia emissions, making animal agriculture more sustainable and climate-friendly.

Empowering Farmers

GreenHeart SE provides training and support to help farmers integrate AniCARE 360 into their operations. Our holistic approach ensures that animal health, environmental stewardship, and farm productivity go hand in hand.
