Materials & Craftsmanship

Each pearl in the Grace Necklace is hand-selected for its luminous quality and perfectly imperfect character, mirroring the beauty in our own unique journeys. The 18k gold-plated chain is hypoallergenic and tarnish-resistant.

The necklace features a secure lobster clasp with an additional 2-inch extender for adjustable wearing length (16-18 inches). Each pearl is hand-knotted using silk thread, a traditional technique that protects the pearls and allows for graceful movement.

Pearl Care Guide

Pearls are organic gems that require special care. Always apply perfumes and cosmetics before wearing your pearls. Gently wipe with a soft cloth after wearing. Store separately from other jewelry to prevent scratching.

The Prayer of Grace

"Heavenly Father, as I wear these pearls, remind me that I am clothed in Your grace and favor. Let their luminescence reflect the light of Christ within me. May this necklace be more than adornment—may it be a tangible reminder that I walk in Your divine favor and purpose. When others notice its beauty, let it open doors to share of Your goodness. In Jesus' name, Amen."

This prayer was handwritten by our founder and prayed over each Grace Collection piece before shipping. You'll receive a printed version with your order.
